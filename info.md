# TVET/CDACC
**TVET Curriculum Development, Assessment, and Certification Council**  

The **TVET/CDACC** oversees competency-based education and training in Kenya. It provides guidelines for assessment, maintains portfolios of evidence, and certifies competence for trainees in TVET institutions, private colleges, and vocational training centers.

## Guidelines on Competency-Based Assessment (CBET)

### 1. Development of Assessment Tools
- Trainers develop assessment tools for various units of competency.  
- Tools are submitted to TVET/CDACC for quality checks.  
- Assessment tools must adhere to CBET guidelines and be customized per course.  
- Approved tools are returned to institutions for use in assessments.

### 2. Maintenance of Portfolio of Evidence (PoE)
- Each trainee has a **Portfolio of Evidence** (PoE) maintained by the department.  
- PoE includes:
  - Marked scripts for written tests  
  - Observation checklists with feedback  
  - Product checklists with feedback  
  - Assessment plans  
  - Audio/video recordings of practical tasks  
- Backups of PoE can be stored digitally (external drives, cloud).  
- Each trainee maintains a personal copy of their PoE.  
- Internal assessors and verifiers maintain assessment and verification records.

### 3. Weighting for Certification
- **Internal assessment:** 60%  
- **External assessment:** 40%  
- Theory vs Practical weighting by KNQF level:

| Level | Practical (%) | Theory (%) |
|-------|---------------|------------|
| 3     | 80            | 20         |
| 4     | 70            | 30         |
| 5     | 60            | 40         |
| 6     | 50            | 50         |

- Passing requirements:
  - Weighted score ≥ 50%  
  - Minimum practical score:
    - Levels 3 & 4: 40%  
    - Levels 5 & 6: 30%  

### 4. Partial Qualifications
- Trainees may enroll for specific unit(s) of competency.  
- Trainers conduct **diagnostic assessment** to determine required foundational units.

### 5. Industrial Attachment/Training
- Industrial attachment is mandatory.  
- Trainees are assessed using mentoring tools in the workplace.  
- Successful completion is required for National Certificate award.

### 6. Components of Portfolio of Evidence (PoE)
- Marked written scripts  
- Written and oral assessment marks sheets  
- Observation checklists  
- Evidence of oral questioning (audio/video)  
- Video/photo clips of practical work or products  
- Assessment plans  
- Signed and dated feedback forms  
- Internal verification reports  
- Filled mentoring tools
