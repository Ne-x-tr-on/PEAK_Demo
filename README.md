# PEAK - Performance Evaluation and Assessment Kit

**PEAK** is a comprehensive digital platform designed to streamline competency-based assessment management for TVET (Technical and Vocational Education and Training) institutions. It helps institutions digitize student portfolios, automate result processing, and track competency achievement — all in one powerful platform.

---

## Features

### Digital Portfolio Management
- Streamlined evidence collection and verification workflow for student assessments.
- Fully digital submission to certification process.
- Reduces administrative paperwork by up to 80%.

### Competency Tracking
- Monitor student progress through continuous assessments and unit competencies.
- Identify at-risk students early to improve outcomes.

### Analytics Dashboard
- Real-time insights into institutional performance and student achievement.
- Makes data-driven decision-making easier.

### Automated Results
- Instantly generate and distribute result slips.
- Secure student access ensures privacy.

### Multi-Campus Support
- Centrally manage multiple institutions, campuses, and departments.
- Perfect for large TVET networks.

### Secure Access
- Enterprise-grade security.
- Role-based permissions and data protection.

---

## Benefits

- **Save Administrative Time:** Automates routine workflows, reducing paperwork drastically.  
- **Improve Student Outcomes:** Real-time monitoring helps educators support students proactively.  
- **CBET Compliant:** Fully compliant with Competency-Based Education and Training requirements.  
- **Paperless Operations:** Go fully digital from assessment submission to certification.

PEAK is tailored specifically for competency-based education, making it the ideal solution for institutions aiming to modernize assessment processes while improving efficiency and student success.
